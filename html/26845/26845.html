はじめに

2014年秋、過去に自分で撮った水中写真を使ってオリジナルの写真集 を出版しようと思い立ちました。

とはいえ私は無名のアマチュア写真家にすぎません。一応、 地球の海フォ トコンテスト に毎年入選できるぐらいにはなったけど、上位入賞の経験 はなし。そのため多くの人に見てもらうには何かしら仕掛けが必要だと 考えました。

そして思いついたのが「日本語名と英語名を併記し、ちょっとした図鑑 風にする」というアイディア。例えば海外では日本語が通じないことが 多いので、我々日本人が現地のガイドや日本人以外のゲストと意思疎通 に使えば便利だろうと。

私の写真の腕前はまだまだですが、楽しんでいただけたら幸いです。

2015年1月　よがたみちあき

Introduction

2014 autumn, I decided to try to publish an original photograph collection book using underwater photos I took.

However, I'm an amateur photographer. Therefore I'm not famous at all. I won the prize in The Blue Earth Underwater Photo Contest every year. But it was a lower rank prize each time. Thus I thought that I need some gimmicks to many people see my photograph collection book.

And I thought that I write a Japanese name and an English name of a creature. In many cases, Japanese is not understood abroad. Therefore It is convenient if there is such a book that for communication with a local dive master and other guests.

I'm not skillful in photography yet. But if you'll enjoy yourself, I'm happy.

January, 2015 YOGATA Michiaki

私の写真の腕前

私の写真の腕前は残念ながら「素人の中では良い方かな」というレベル。 まだ勘と運に頼った部分が多く、この本にも不思議な絞りとシャッター スピードの組み合わせで撮った写真が何枚も含まれています。

陸上の撮影では重要なフラッシュ光の当てかたについても水中では注意 がいかないことが多いです。

よって水中写真の経験が浅い人や苦手な人には良い写真に見えても、 プロの写真家や熟練者からすればあれこれ注文をつけたくなるでしょ う。

でも、腕前が未熟でも、時にまぐれ当たりで印象的な絵が撮れたりする のも写真の楽しさですよね。

The skill of my photography

Unfortunately the skill of my photography is just good as an amateur. Several photos on this book were taken by feeling and luck. Thus these photos had taken by strange aperture and shutter speed.

And the lighting is very important for photography. But I'm not taking good care so much in the water.

Many of my photos seem to good pictures for beginners. But skilled photographer may not be satisfied with my photos.

However, it's pleasure of the photography that I can take a striking photo accidentally even if my skill is immature.

2014年の4月、 インドネシアのアンボン島 に出かけました。ニューギ ニア島の西隣、沖縄本島から3,200kmぐらい南にある島です。

ただしマルクダイバーズの客層はほとんどが欧米人ダイバー。よってゲ ストもスタッフもみな英語を話します。満足に意思疎通ができないのは 私だけでした。

それでもダイビングは世界共通。ブリーフィングが英語でもガイドの言 わんとすることはだいたい理解できます。

とはいえ「Flamboyant Cuttlefish（フランボイアントカトルフィッ シュ）」や「Bobtail Squid（ボブテイルスクイード）」などと言われても、 それが何を示すのかが解りません。カトルフィッシュもスクイードもイ カってことは知っていたけど、何イカなのか見当がつきませんでした。

そんなわけで和名と英名が併記された図鑑があれば便利だと思い、この 写真集で採用しました。

Necessity of Japanese name and English name

In April of 2014, I went to Ambon island in Indonesia . Ambon is next to the west of New Guinea. It's about 3,200km of south island from Okinawa in Japan.

I used a Maluku Divers in the island. They're managing diving service, accommodations and a restaurant. They had a substantial camera room, and the meal was also good. Their homemade sambal source was a delicious in particular.

But, most guests of Maluku Divers were European diver and American diver. Therefore every guests and staff spoke English. But I didn't. I could only minimal communication.

Still the diving is worldwide. I understood almost all thing a dive master was talking about at a briefing in English.

However, When he said "Flamboyant Cuttlefish" and "Bobtail Squid", I didn't know what that indicates. I knew that a cuttlefish and squid were a cephalopod. But I didn't know these are what species.

So, I thought it is convenient if there is a biological encyclopedia that the name of the creature has been written in Japanese and English. Thus I adopted the idea in this book.

想定する使い方

例えば日本人ダイバーが海外のダイビングサービスを使う際、見たい生 物を現地ガイドにリクエストするために英語名を言えるといいですよ ね。

もちろん、その場合は図鑑を交えて意思疎通すればいいのだけど、ダイ ビング前のブリーフィングはだいたい船の上で行われるので、わざわざ 図鑑を持ち込むのは面倒です。濡れたらページがふやけるし。

逆に海外のダイビングサービスが日本人のゲストを受け入れる際、現地 ガイドが生物の日本語名を言えるとサービスの質が上がります。

よって日本人が英語名を覚える用途と、日本人を受け入れる現地ガイド が日本語名を覚える用途にこの本を使ってもらえると嬉しいです。

なお、学術的な図鑑ではないので学名は省きました。

Assumed usage

For example, when Japanese divers use a local diving services abroad, they should want to request the name of the creature which they want to see in English.

Of course, they can communicate using biological encyclopedia. But It's troublesome to bring a book. Because a briefing before diving is often performed on the boat. If the book get wet, the book will damaged.

Further, When overseas diving service accepts a Japanese guest, if local dive master can tell a Japanese name of a creature, the quality of the service will improve.

Therefore, I'm glad If Japanese diver uses this book to memorize an English name of a creature. And also if a local dive master who accepts Japanese diver use to memorize a Japanese name of a creature.

But, I omitted a scientific name because this is not an academic book.

The purpose of this photograph book

I published this photograph book. Because I hope that a lot of people look at this book, and I wants some opportunity.

But if I cause a boom of the original photograph book, I can receive a request of publication support from other people. That would be my sideline.

Or I'd like to make this book a herald on e-book of photograph collection book like encyclopedia. That's written in Japanese and English. It's because I'd like to know the Japanese name of the creature and English name after taking creature's photograph.

I have the other plans. But I'd like to show them on different chance.

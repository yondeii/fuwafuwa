honkon ha yorude ryougaeyaga aitenaku,shoushou temadottaga jidouno ryougaekide kane get sita. makudi de tyuumon suruto,ba-ga- igaiha jakikomerareta. suta- bakus ha samon no sando ga jakihidokattaga,salada ha sonna jakinakatta.salada tabeta. oyaga tyugokuhndoni kaneokutta toyuunide, honkon no tikakuno tosini ikukotonisita.

kono toside kaneuketetorouto sitaga,kanari temadotta. honkinrestrande mesikuttaga,nu-doruha sonna jakinakattaga,utuwa toka hasiga nanka hidokatta,.

kaneuketorutame,nankenka bank ittaga,yunion yatteorazu,kekkou aruite,kekkyoku dokokano china bank ni ittaga,kaneuketoruniha kurejitto ka-do ga hutuyouto iwarete uketorenakatta. atode kiduitaga mottotikakuni betuno china ginkouatta. takusi-mo nandoka tukattaga,bakanisitayouna taido tottari,tyugokugode donattekite nande oreno yuukotoga wakaranainda mitaku yuttekitarisita. kouyuuyaturaha sekainiha iroirona gengoga arutoyuuktowo siranaiyoudearu. juusuuken mawattanotini,aru bank de oneesan sutahhuga kanari nenwokomete sagyou sitekurete,kurejitto ka-do naside kaneuketoru kotogadekita.tyuugokuniha tamani zennin mo irurasii. ga sagyou owarukuraini keibiinga jakimede nirandekita.

kaneuketottaato,tikakuno restran suuken mawattaga,dokomo kanjiwaruku,makau poi restrande tyuumonsitaga,jakikomerarete hanbunkuraisika kuenakatta. yadoha kantanni toretanode,restran sae matomo nara kokoha kanari iitokokamo sirenai.

tyugokuuinha jibunraha kanji kakerukara taminzokuyori sugureterutoka omotteru youdaga,kanjidekakouga tadano hyouon moji de kakouga, kakareteru Naiyou ga daijinanodeatre, kanjide kakarete aruhouga sugureteruto mounoha,kdaimousoudarou.tasikani kanjide kakaretearuto,nanikasirano seisin wo kanjirukomosirenaiga,soudatositemo kanjigatukuraretanoha tagengowo bakanisuru tamejanaidarou. imano tyugokujinha kanji parisaijinga ooiyoudearu.

tyugokujinha zentaishugisha rasiku, taninni taisuru sonkeisintokaha mottenaiyoude,mottetatositemo ma,jibunto onajikuraidaro mitaina yuganda yatumo iruyouda. daga,kanjide kakunoga toutoku,hokanogengo bakanisiteruyouna yatuha,tadano bakadato ieru. ningendaresimo onajitoka iinagara, naisin shouzensetu totte,tyugokuinha zennin,taminzokuha gokuaku to mousin siteru yatumo ooiyoudearu. zentaishugitekini ningenbyoudouwo tokumonoha Sitoni taisiteha nanno sonkeisinmo motazu,hituzentekini hankirisuto dearu.

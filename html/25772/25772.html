 Once upon a time there lived in England a very energetic boy called Oliver. He was naturally curious about new things.   

    

 One day, Oliver went to the church in his neighborhood after school because this church was very famous for a box. The box has been called the "Ark of the Covenant." Rumors had been circulated that there were two pieces of stones inside inscribed with the Ten Commandments of God and it should NEVER be touched. However, Oliver wanted to touch it to find out what was actually inside the box.   

    

 It looked like there was nobody about when he went into the church anxiously. He could see the sacred areas walking along the long nave, which was the holiest place in the church. Then, Oliver noticed there was a small box behind some sculptures, and shouted "this is it!" Oliver quickly opened the cover and looked into the box. Suddenly he fell deeply asleep when the magic seal was broken.  

    

 When Oliver woke out of the sleep, he realized he was in the church that looked similar to his local church. There was a sculpture of St. Michael in front of him. Oliver looked up at his face and gazed at St. Michael. All of a sudden St. Michael spoke to him!  

    

 "Hello, I am Archangel Michael," said St. Michael.  

 Oliver was dazed with astonishment.  

 "You are in the St. Michael Cathedral in Paris now, but you are in the dream," said St. Michael.  

 "Why am I here now?" asked Oliver.  

 "You touched the Ark of the Covenant, didn't you?" said St. Michael.  

 "Yes, but I still doubt if that box is really a magic one," said Oliver.  

    

 When Oliver was talking to St. Michael, he heard footsteps coming towards them. And then, someone in black said to him, "I am Pierre and am serving as a priest in this Cathedral."  

    

 Oliver apologized to him, "I am sorry I crept in!"   

 "Don't worry about it. You are seeing me and St. Michael today," said Pierre.  

 "What do you mean?" said Oliver.  

 St. Michael showed Oliver a magic sword inscribed with the symbol of a pentagram.  

    

 "This sword is spelled by magic, and it gives comfort and happiness, on the other hand, it also brings harm and misfortune. There could be conflict over the sword when you grow old. So, keep this sword a secret."   

 "Why do you tell me about this story?" said Oliver.  

 "You have a role to deliver messages to people from God."  

 "Is this a message from God as well?"   

 "Of course"  

 "But......it's difficult to believe!"  

 "When you awake from this dream, you will find a stone of Lazurite in your pocket. It is a magic stone. If you fit this stone into the center of the symbol of the pentagram inscribed on the sword, your dream will come true. This stone is your talisman and never pass it to someone who asked you about the sword," said St. Michael.  

    

 There actually was a stone of Lazurite in his pocket when Oliver awoke from his dream. He was excited and went back home in a hurry with the stone.   

 When Oliver turned on TV and watched for a while, he was shocked again that St. Michael Cathedral in Paris was shown on the screen.   

 "The sword inscribed with the symbol of pentagram is missing through fire!" a reporter said.   

 At this time, Oliver thought it was a true story.   

    

    

 The End
